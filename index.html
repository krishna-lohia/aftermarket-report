FIIs dump ₹3,255 Cr, the heaviest single-day exit in three weeks as Nifty craters to 24,173

Markets Today

Nifty fell 0.84% to 24,173, Sensex lost 1.09%, Bank Nifty dropped 1.43%. The selloff was sharp and narrow: 342 stocks declined against 158 advances, and VIX climbed 1.6% to 18.6. FII / DII Flows

FIIs sold ₹3,255 Cr net today, their fourth consecutive session of selling and the heaviest exit since early April. DIIs bought ₹941 Cr, a fraction of the ₹2,967 Cr they deployed on Monday. For the first time since March, both FIIs and DIIs sold together yesterday, and today FIIs doubled down while DII support evaporated.
