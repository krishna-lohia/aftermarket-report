Nifty is above both the 21-EMA (23,807.74) and 50-EMA (23,858.17) — near and medium-term trends intact. A close below 23,807.74 would be the first warning.

VIX fell 2.84% to 13.0. Below 15 is the calm zone. Good for bulls, though complacency can bite.
