Banks rip 2.6%, Nifty closes above both EMAs as crude crashes 9.4%

Markets Today

Nifty gained 298 points (1.24%) to 24,330, powered by a 2.63% surge in Bank Nifty to 55,981 and a parallel 2.6% lift in the Midcap index to 14,312. Breadth was lopsided: 408 advances against just 92 declines across the Nifty 500. VIX shed 6.87% to 16.68, its lowest close since the FII exodus began in late April.
