Nifty edges up 0.37% to 24,265.05

Markets Today

Nifty 50 gained 89 points (+0.37%) to close at 24,265.05. Sensex mirrored the move, gained 0.33% to 77,755.18.

Bank Nifty underperformed — fell 0.23% to 57,896.7, 0.6% behind Nifty. Banking weakness can reflect concern about interest margins or credit stress.

Breadth was poor — 260 declines vs 240 advances out of 500 stocks. More stocks fell than rose — the damage was wider than the headline number suggests.

The rupee strengthened to 95.49 against the dollar (-0.14%).

VIX fell 3.42% to 11.9. Below 15 is the calm zone. Good for bulls, though complacency can bite.
