Steady gains across the board — Nifty adds 0.63% to 24,031.65

Markets Today

Nifty 50 gained 150 points (+0.63%) to close at 24,031.65. Sensex mirrored the move, gained 0.65% to 76,998.74. VIX fell 9.74% to 13.3.

Bank Nifty outperformed — gained 1.13% to 57,382.3, 0.5% ahead of Nifty. Banking strength is often a sign of confidence in credit and economic momentum.

Breadth was positive — 425 advances vs 75 declines out of 500 stocks. At 5.7:1, gains were broad-based, not just index heavyweights.

Nifty is above both the 21-EMA (23,988.81) and 50-EMA (23,935.98) — near and medium-term trends intact. A close below 23,988.81 would be the first warning.

VIX fell 9.74% to 13.3. Below 15 is the calm zone. Good for bulls, though complacency can bite.
