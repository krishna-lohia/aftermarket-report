Broad rally lifts Nifty past 24,200 as FIIs return — all 12 sectors in green

Market Overview

Nifty50 closed at 24,231.30, up 388.65 points (1.63%), while Sensex gained 1.64%. The move was decisive: 7,782 advances to just 827 declines tells you how wide the rally ran. VIX collapsed 8.93% to 18.67, signaling a sharp drop in nervousness. Banks lagged the party — Nifty Bank gained only 1.25%, while IT (2.84%) and Realty (2.56%) led the charge.

Institutional Flows

FIIs turned net buyers for the second time in four days, inflow of 666.15 Cr after selling 1,983.20 Cr just two days back. DIIs, however, net sold 568.98 Cr — domestic flows stayed cautious even as global money stepped in. The rupee held steady at 93.50, and crude oil inched up just 0.06%, so no currency or commodity headwinds to worry about today.
