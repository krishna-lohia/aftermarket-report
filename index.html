Nifty edges up 0.83% to 24,021.65

Markets Today

Nifty 50 gained 198 points (+0.83%) to close at 24,021.65. Sensex mirrored the move, gained 1.04% to 76,991.22.

Bank Nifty outperformed — gained 1.69% to 58,150.35, 0.9% ahead of Nifty. Banking strength is often a sign of confidence in credit and economic momentum.

Breadth was positive — 263 advances vs 235 declines out of 500 stocks. A 1.1:1 ratio — decent but not overwhelming participation.

Commodities

Crude oil fell 4.55% to ₹6,647 per barrel. That is good news for India, which imports around 85% of its crude oil requirements. Cheaper oil reduces the import bill, takes pressure off the rupee, and brings down fuel costs across the economy.

Gold fell 3.21% to ₹1,41,825/10g; silver similarly dropped 3.32% to ₹2,18,335/kg.

The rupee weakened to 92.87 against the dollar (+0.00%).

Options Activity

Options positioning: resistance at 25,000 (heaviest call OI), support at 24,000 (heaviest put OI). PCR at 1.2 is mildly bullish — more traders are writing puts, comfortable selling downside protection.

Nifty is above both the 21-EMA (23,778.75) and 50-EMA (23,848.38) — near and medium-term trends intact. A close below 23,778.75 would be the first warning.

VIX fell 3.95% to 13.4. Below 15 is the calm zone. Good for bulls, though complacency can bite.
