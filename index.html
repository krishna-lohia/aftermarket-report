Commodities

Crude oil fell 5.91% to ₹7,596 per barrel. That is good news for India, which imports around 85% of its crude oil requirements. Cheaper oil reduces the import bill, takes pressure off the rupee, and brings down fuel costs across the economy.

Gold rose 2.03% to ₹1,53,580/10g. Investors often move into gold when uncertainty rises, making this move worth watching; silver similarly rose 2.78% to ₹2,53,024/kg.

The rupee strengthened to 94.73 against the dollar (-0.54%). A stronger rupee is good for importers but squeezes the margins of exporters like IT and pharma firms.
