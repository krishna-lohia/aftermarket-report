Steady gains across the board — Nifty adds 0.94% to 24,188.2

Markets Today

Nifty 50 gained 225 points (+0.94%) to close at 24,188.2. Sensex mirrored the move, gained 1.03% to 77,534.34. VIX fell 7.86% to 12.3.

Bank Nifty outperformed — gained 1.36% to 58,031, 0.4% ahead of Nifty. Banking strength is often a sign of confidence in credit and economic momentum.

Breadth was positive — 412 advances vs 87 declines out of 500 stocks. At 4.7:1, gains were broad-based, not just index heavyweights.

Options Activity

Options positioning: resistance at 24,500 (heaviest call OI), support at 24,200 (heaviest put OI). PCR at 1.34 is bullish — more traders are writing puts, comfortable selling downside protection.

Nifty is above both the 21-EMA (24,001.46) and 50-EMA (23,943.35) — near and medium-term trends intact. A close below 24,001.46 would be the first warning.

VIX fell 7.86% to 12.3. Below 15 is the calm zone. Good for bulls, though complacency can bite.
